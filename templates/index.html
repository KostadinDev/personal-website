Motional EMF (ε) due to Compression in Ellipses

Computational Physics Numerical Analysis

This project studies the motional emf (ε) generated by the compression of an elliptical conducting loop traversed by a constant magnetic field. A numerical algorithm with full 128-bit precision is developed and real-world applications are discussed.

Glad to have worked on this project together with Dr. Phillipe Binder and Alexa Runyan.

Co-Learning: Interactive Annotation Tool with DL

Computer Vision Deep Learning HCI

Co-Learning is human-in-the-loop AI system for interactive annotation built on top of LabelMe. The AI and human learn from each other to make annotation quick and painless. We test it on aerial images of Hawaii's vegetation taken by drones.

Thanks to Dr. Travis Mandel and Edward Cashmane for working with me on this project and to Maxwell Panoff for his contribution.
